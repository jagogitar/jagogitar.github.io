24. Buat dialog complaining berdasarkan situasi dibawah ini! you are travelling to guadalajara and need two trains to get there. you have already bought the tickets, and you have twenty minutes from the moment you get to station on one train until you take the second one. It's more than enough time. However, the first train arrives late at the station and you miss the second one. You are very upset.


Expressing Complaint

Passenger: I’m sorry but I’d like to make a complaint about your train time schedule.

CS: Yes ma’am, please describe your situation.

Passenger: So I bought tickets to Guadalajara, there are two trips. Everything was okay until your first train arrived late which means that I lost my second train! How is that possible? I have already counted the timing so carefully but my trip schedule went to a mess because of your company’s mistake. Is there anything that can be done to improve the situation?

CS: First of all we are very sorry for your situation and we’ll do our best not to make the same mistake again. As our apologize, we will offer you the second trip that you missed with another train. It will be in executive class, also we will give you a refund for the ticket you have bought earlier. Again, we are very sorry for this.

Passenger: Alright, thanks a lot. I really hope your company will do improvements in the future.

CS: Thank you ma’am for your suggestion, and we will try our best.

Pembahasan

Complaints (protes) adalah ungkapan yang menunjukan rasa tidak senang atau kesal sebagai respon dari sesuatu yang tidak menyenangkan. Ada banyak situasi dan keadaan yang sering membuat seseorang kesal. Berikut ini adalah contoh ungkapan yang bisa kamu ucapkan ketika ingin protes secara sopan:

I’m sorry to say this but…I’m afraid I’ve got a complaint about….Excuse me, I’m afraid…..Look, I’m sorry to trouble you, but….There seems to be a problem with….Excuse me if I am out of line, but…..Maybe you forgot to….I’m sorry but I’d like to make a complaint about…Is there anything that can be done to improve the situation?How can we fix this?

Selanjutnya adalah bagaimana respon yang tepat untuk menanggapi sebuah complaint:

I’m so sorry but this will never happen againI can’t tell you how sorry I amI’m really sorry; we’ll do our best not to do the same mistake again.
