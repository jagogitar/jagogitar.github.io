1. Let it be, let it be, let it be, lrt it be Whisper word of wisdom Let it be (Let It Be, by The Beatles) The verse above uses . . . Pilihan jawaban A. alliteration B. simile C. hyperbole D. metaphor E. assonance


Jawaban:

Let it be, let it be, let it be, let it be

Whisper word of wisdom

Let it be

(Let It Be, by The Beatles)

The verse above uses assonance (E).

Penjelasan:

A. alliteration : the occurrence of the same letter or sound at the beginning of adjacent or closely connected words.

B. simile :  a figure of speech that compares two different things in an interesting way. A simile uses the words "like" or "as" to draw a comparison.

C. Hyperbole: a figure of speech that uses extreme exaggeration to make a point or show emphasis. It is the opposite of understatement.

D. Metaphor: a metaphor simply states the comparison without using "like" or "as".

E. Assonance: the repetition of vowel sounds in nearby words.

c.kunci F adalah tanda yg menunjukan nada f pada garis keempat dari paranada.

22. apa figurative language dari lagu " let it be by The Beatles "​


The only figurative language I recognize in it is that McCartney is not necessarily talking about “Mother Mary” in the religious sense of the mother of Jesus. His own mother was named Mary (who died when he was young), so it carries a double meaning for him. I think that is typical Beatles doublespeak.

The rest of the lyrics are straightforward, referring to conflict and resolution personally and in the world in general.
